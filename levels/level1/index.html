Mr. Jones flees through the five-barred gate. The animals stand panting in the yard. Inside the great barn, Snowball and Napoleon climb a ladder, brushes in their trotters, and paint upon the wall the unalterable laws by which all the animals on Animal Farm must live for ever after.
