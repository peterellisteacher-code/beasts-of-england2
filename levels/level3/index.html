    The Battle of the Cowshed

    Mr. Jones and his men return with sticks and a gun. They have been at The Red Lion. Snowball studied an old book of Julius Caesar's campaigns from the farmhouse.

    The battle has three waves:

    1. Pigeons and geese harass and demoralise the men.
    2. The feigned retreat — withdraw at exactly the right moment.
    3. The ambush at the cowshed — Boxer's killing strike.

    For each wave: when an enemy enters the strike zone, press SPACE (or click STRIKE). Hit too early, miss. Hit too late, the cowshed takes damage.
